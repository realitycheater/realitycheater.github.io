about me

I'm matt, I also go by "doki" on various parts of the internet because I had the bright idea to let other people choose my online alias when I was 17.

I mainly spend my time grinding FPS games and trying to make videos out of the clips I get, I am somewhat good at Kovaaks and was somehow also a top 100 osu! player in a previous life.

I also run aimmag, a video magazine styled after 411vm focusing on the aim and peripheral scene, which is currently on hiatus.

Offline I am a proud Hongkonger, a computer engineering graduate and a full-time software developer. I also have other interests like coffee, vinyl records and postmodern philosophy.
